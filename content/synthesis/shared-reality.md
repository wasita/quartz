# shared reality

shared reality refers to the experience of having in common with others' inner states about the world. it is a foundational concept for understanding social cognition, group dynamics, and interpersonal influence.

## key sources

- [[higgins2021shared]] — Higgins' comprehensive theory of shared reality as a fundamental human motivation

## core ideas

- shared reality is not mere agreement on facts, but the experience of sharing inner states (beliefs, feelings, evaluations) with specific others
- it serves as a basic human motivation alongside other social motives
- when shared reality breaks down between groups, it can drive polarization and conflict
